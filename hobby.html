My Hobby: Exploring Music

One of my favorite things to do is listen to different types of music. Music has helped me in many ways—relaxing my mind, inspiring creativity, and even improving my focus during studying. I love discovering new sounds, instruments, and rhythms from all around the world.

Taemin's "Stone Heart" is a song about being trapped in an illusion of love and longing after a painful heartbreak.

Doja Cat's "Streets" is a song about longing to return to a former romantic partner and the difficulty of finding someone as unique as them.

DENȲ and Arthur Nery's "Sabik" is a Tagalog word meaning eager or craving, is a soulful R&B ballad that delves into the themes of intense longing, unrequited or complicated love, and the struggle to let go.

Penomeco's "Shy (eh o)" is a track that sees the artist experimenting with Afro-pop influences and is essentially about vying for someone's attention and expressing a strong, undeniable attraction.

Chase Atlantic's "TRIGGERED" explores themes of intense desire, obsession, and a potentially toxic or destructive relationship.

ALPHA's "Seni Oilai" (which translates to "Thinking of You" or "I Think of You" from Kazakh, as ALPHA is a Kazakh boyband) is a song about an intense and almost obsessive focus on a past love, despite the relationship being over or the person being with someone else.
